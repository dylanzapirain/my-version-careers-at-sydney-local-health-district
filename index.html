What is working at Sydney Local Health District like?

Working at Sydney Local Health District is incredibly rewarding. We are responsible for the health and wellbeing of more than 750,000 people living within the inner west and CBD of Sydney, as well as many more from rural and remote parts of NSW and Australia.

Sydney Local Health District is made up of hospitals and health services across:

  • The City of Sydney
  • Inner West Council
  • Canterbury-Bankstown
  • Canada Bay
  • Burwood
  • Strathfield

If you recieved your COVID-19 vacciniation from the former Mass Vaccination Centre at Sydney Olympic park you've met us before. Our experience and expertise helped us to establish and operate the Olympic Park Vaccination Centre delivering over 1 million Pfizer, Moderna, AstraZenca and Noavax vaccines to help protect you from COVID-19.

Welcome to Sydney Local Health District. Our vision is excellence in health and healthcare for all.

World-class education and development

Robust training and development opportunities are available to our staff through the district education provider Centre for Education and Workforce Development (CEWD). SLHD Nursing and Midwifery services work in close collaboration with CEWD to provide opportunities for education in key career directions including

Governance, Engagement, Innovation, clinical skills, management, leadership, education, research and technical skills.

Research and innovation opportunities

We are always actively involved in a number of exciting research projects and initiatives. We celebrate all successes - whether large or small. SLHD invests in research and clinical innovation and celebrates clinical excellence and all our clinical leaders.

The District is widely recognised as a leader in research, education and in developing innovative models of care, with highlyskilled staff and mature clinical services.

Our locations

Our hospitals and facilities span across an area of approximately 126 square kilometres. Made up of the Local Government Areas of the City of Sydney, Inner West Council, Canterbury-Bankstown, Canada Bay, Burwood and Strathfield.
